# Wave 0 Synthesis — Grounded Node Map + First Kills

*2026-07-05. Synthesis of 6 grounding memos (engineer + supply-chain × W/R/AV). This is the foundation Wave 1 prices.*

## The dominant truth (all three tracks agree)
**~60–80% of every dollar raised flows through the Nvidia/TSMC/HBM mega-cap compute stack** — and synthetic data *increases* GPU spend, it doesn't reduce it. That spend is real and forced but **cannot multiply a small-cap's revenue and is already priced.** So the mega-cap compute node is a KILL *for our specific thesis* (no asymmetry), even though it's the biggest forced-spend bucket. Our edge lives in the **non-compute physical nodes** where forced spend reaches accessible small/mid-caps that haven't re-rated.
