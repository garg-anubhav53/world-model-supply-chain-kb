- **3m/6m/12m %**: Approximate, derived from 52-week high/low ranges and YTD data points. Where YTD was the only data point, 3m and 6m are estimates interpolated from momentum context. Mark all %-moves as **estimates** unless sourced to a specific dated article.

- **NOT-MOVED** flag on SMCI is idiosyncratic (legal/operational), not valuation-driven.
- MU listed as PARTIAL because forward multiple (6.8x) is genuinely cheap on supercycle earnings — the stock has run but may not be fully priced on a normalized-cycle basis.
- AVGO listed as NOT-MOVED (de-rated) because it's 27% off its 52w high and fwd multiple has compressed significantly — thesis (custom ASICs + networking) intact but price has gone sideways/down.
