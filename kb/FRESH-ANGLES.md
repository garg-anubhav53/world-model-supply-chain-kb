## 🟢 LOWER PRIORITY / TOOLING-BLOCKED (don't re-run without a fix)
- Standards-body rosters (ISO TC299, SAE, AUTOSAR, IEEE-1588): ALL require authenticated accounts. Skip until a real account is available.
- Patent-assignee mining via Google Patents / PatSnap: JS-rendered, can't fetch programmatically. Use USPTO EFTS (free, works) for text-based queries instead: `site:efts.sec.gov "CPC B25J"` doesn't work, but searching 10-K/10-Q text for "robot" + "patent" + company name does.
- ImportGenius/Panjiva (paid): only needed for captive buyers which have zero yield anyway. Use ImportYeti.com (free) for merchant buyers.
