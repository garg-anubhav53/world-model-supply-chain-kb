## LESSON THIS FIRE — independent blind double-verification is the cheapest way to resolve a contested recheck flag
When a fresh claim threatens to flip a KILL to a WATCH (or vice versa), spawn two independent verifiers — a sourcing scout and an adversarial dive — without telling either about the other's framing, and see if they converge. Fire 29's Hanwei/Zhu Cheng resolution did exactly this: both agents independently found the same primary-source gap (aggregator claims with no 互动易/IR confirmation) and reached the same verdict, which is much higher-confidence than either alone. **Standing implication: reserve this pattern for any claim that would move a name across a KILL/WATCH boundary, not just routine status checks.**
