## LESSON THIS FIRE — a genuine consortium "founding member" can still mean zero product relationship
SRE Holdings is a real, verifiable founding member of Japan's KyoHA humanoid consortium — but every source describing its actual role calls it a financial/strategic backer, not a component supplier. This is a distinct failure-mode from the already-known ARRK Corporation trap (a weaker "sponsor tier" with generic services language) — here the membership tier is genuine (founding member) but the ROLE itself is capital, not hardware. **Standing implication: before diligencing any consortium-member lead, first find a source describing the member's actual described role (capital backer vs. component/technology supplier), not just its membership tier or how prominently it's listed.**
