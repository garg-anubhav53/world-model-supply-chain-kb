Root cause is structural, not a search failure: none of these OEMs has an established enough bank-credit/facility history to run a securitization program — Unitree only got STAR Market registration in July 2026, UBTech (the longest-listed, 2.5yr) prefers direct equity acquisition of suppliers over receivables-ABS, the rest are pre-IPO. Worth revisiting in 12-18 months, not before.
