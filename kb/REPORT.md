## Sourcing: two methods validated, one lead exposed a data-quality lesson
- **SEC EDGAR full-text search** was technically validated this fire — a prior read that the UI was "inaccessible" turned out to be wrong; the efts.sec.gov JSON backend is directly reachable via a simple authenticated request. Buyer-name-paired queries ("Zoox," "Apptronik," "Boston Dynamics" + "strategic supplier"/"design win") are productive; generic "significant customer" phrasing (tried back in Fire 8) is not. The method re-found CVGI/Zoox — already known and already-killed in this KB — which at least confirms the technique surfaces real disclosures; the practical problem is that the market tends to reprice these fast, so future passes should restrict to the most recent 60-90 days to try to get ahead of the re-rating rather than behind it.
- **Customs/bill-of-lading reuse** was tried on 5 untested US/EU-deployed merchant AV fleets (Aurora, Kodiak, Nuro, Motional, Gatik) per Fire 20's own recommendation. Nuro turned out to be a genuinely productive merchant buyer with real, dated import records — but every named supplier was either too large/diversified to move the needle (Pegatron, HL Mando, BYD) or, in the "Cooler Master" case, misidentified entirely. Aurora, Kodiak, and Motional could not be resolved either way this fire — ImportGenius/ImportYeti increasingly gate their search function behind a login, and guessed URL slugs didn't resolve correctly (one even landed on an unrelated same-named company). These three are logged as genuinely INCONCLUSIVE, not dead-ends — worth a real retry with the actual search interface, not a repeat of the guessed-slug approach.
- **New method piloted: idle-dimension trigger scan.** Instead of a full re-sweep of every previously-tested-negative dimension, this fire ran a fast, targeted fresh-trigger check across four of them at once (marketplace/data, recycling, mining/construction automation, semicap equipment). One fired (marketplace/data — Momenta Global's IPO), three stayed correctly idle. This is a cheaper way to keep dormant dimensions honest without spending a full scout's budget on each one — worth reusing whenever several dimensions are simultaneously idle.
