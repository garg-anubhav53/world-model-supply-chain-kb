# Zen Technologies — ZENTEC (NSE, India)
Updated 2026-07-07 (Fire 10) | Verdict: **WATCH (early-stage, new Fire 10)** | Track: **R (defense-robotics)**

- **What:** India defense-simulation company holding a 45.33% stake in Bhairav Robotics (quadrupedal robots + "Rakshak" autonomous weapon station). Core business = training simulators + counter-drone (C-UAS) systems for the Indian military.
- **Price (2-source, Jul 6 2026):** ₹1,834.8 (aggregator) / ₹1,853 (screener.in). 12m move: -9.28% / -4% — **NOT overrun**, actually down over the period despite order-book growth.
- **Gate 1 — NOT re-rated:** PASS. Flat-to-down 12m on both sources.
- **Gate 2 — Causal link:** PASS on the anti-drone segment, NOT YET on Bhairav. Named buyer = Indian Ministry of Defence, ₹404cr C-UAS + training-simulator order (Jan 16 2026, confirmed across BusinessToday/Kotak Neo/Whalesbook/ScanX), part of a real drumbeat (₹37cr Oct'25, ₹108cr Nov'25, ₹120cr Dec'25, ₹404cr Jan'26). Bhairav Robotics itself (the humanoid/quadrupedal angle) has **no disclosed standalone revenue** — still pre-revenue optionality, not yet a contributor.
- **Gate 3 — Revenue multiplication:** MATERIAL but base is smaller than press implies. FY26 consolidated revenue **₹688cr** (screener.in + multibagg/investywise, 2-source) — note this is DOWN 29% YoY from ₹974cr FY25, and contradicts an earlier ₹773cr figure; use ₹688cr. The ₹404cr order = ~59% of one year's revenue and ~30% of the ₹1,336cr order book — genuinely large if it converts on schedule.
- **Gate 4 — Financial health:** MIXED. Positives: debt-free (₹19cr borrowings vs ~₹966cr net cash), 37-42% operating margins, ROCE ~45%. Red flags: revenue fell 29% YoY despite order growth; working-capital days ballooned 283→543 (execution/collection stress typical of government contracting); ₹86.4cr "other income" propping up reported profit; promoter holding diluted 60.2%→48.5% over 3 years; valuation already rich (~87x P/E, ~8.9x book) — flat price masks that the market hasn't left this cheap.
- **Capture-rate — the binding gate:** UNFAVORABLE for a clean BUY. NOT sole-source: competitive field includes BEL (state-owned, ~₹572cr IDDIS tranche, volume leader), Big Bang Boom Solutions (₹200cr+ via iDEX), Paras Defence, Indrajaal, Adani Defence. India C-UAS market ~₹120bn ($1.6bn) over 5yrs, growing ~28%/yr — large, multi-vendor. Zen is a leading private player, not a monopoly.
- **Adversarial bear case:** FY26 revenue actually contracted 29% despite an accelerating order book — execution/collection problems (543-day working capital) mean orders aren't reliably converting to revenue on schedule, undermining the "multiply" thesis regardless of headline wins. Stock already at ~87x earnings — any delivery slippage, MoD budget reallocation, or competitive loss to BEL/Big Bang Boom could de-rate rather than re-rate. Bhairav Robotics is a minority (45%) stake in an unproven, pre-revenue business — speculative optionality, not a financial catalyst today.
- **Verdict: WATCH, not BUY.** Real named-buyer link and order materiality on the anti-drone segment, but valuation already rich, revenue execution deteriorating, and capture-rate is multi-vendor. Bhairav Robotics (the actual humanoid/quadrupedal angle) remains unquantified optionality. Flip toward BUY: FY27 Q1-Q2 revenue re-acceleration against the order book, resolution of the working-capital blowout, a single large program win clarifying true capture-rate vs BEL/Big Bang Boom, or a first disclosed Bhairav Robotics revenue line.
- **Sources:** screener.in (price + FY26 financials) · BusinessToday/Kotak Neo/Whalesbook/ScanX (₹404cr MoD order, Jan 2026) · MarketsMojo (working-capital, other-income detail) · ScanX FY26 concall summary.
