# Jiangsu Leili Motor Co. (300660.SZ, Shenzhen A-share)
Last updated: 2026-07-06 (Fire 6, new WATCH). Track: R (humanoid/quadruped robotics).

## What they sell
Hollow-cup motors, linear-motion modules, frameless torque motors, joint modules — core actuator-tier components for humanoid/quadruped robot joints. Also a diversified base business: home appliance motors, auto-parts motors (incl. LiDAR/AC-compressor motors), industrial-control motors.

## Named forced buyer (Gate 1 — SATISFIED)
Official investor-relations disclosure (特定对象调研 filing #2025-007, dated 2025-10-30, disclosed 2025-10-31, hosted by board secretary/CFO 殷成龙; PDF: https://pdf.dfcfw.com/pdf/H2_AN202510311772566979_1.pdf):
> "在机器人核心零部件方面，公司及子公司与蔚蓝科技、智元机器人等客户已形成稳定订单，今年前三季度公司空心杯电机、线性运动模组、无框力矩电机、关节模组等产品均已实现小批量出货。"
— i.e. Leili has formed **"stable orders"** with **Zhiyuan Robotics/AgiBot** (well-funded humanoid OEM, ~RMB1.05B 2025 revenue, 2026 HK IPO planned) and **Wellblue Technology / 蔚蓝科技** (quadruped "robot dog" maker), already at small-batch shipment across four product lines. A separate 互动易/10jqka disclosure (2025-08-11) independently corroborates the Wellblue relationship: "公司与南京蔚蓝科技建立战略合作关系，为其提供四足机器人关节执行器" (joint actuators for quadruped robots).

## Revenue base & multiplication math
9M-2025 total revenue RMB3.008B (+21.5% YoY) — growth currently driven by appliances, auto-parts (+52%, LiDAR/AC-compressor motors), industrial control (+31%, N. American industrial motors). Robotics is NOT yet quantified/broken out — described only qualitatively as "small-batch shipment," implying low-single-digit % of revenue today. If Zhiyuan/AgiBot scales toward its planned HK IPO and production ramp, and Leili is a qualified/preferred actuator vendor, robotics could become a material, fast-growing new revenue line on top of a currently non-robotics base — genuine multiplication optionality, but NOT YET priced into disclosed financials.

## Vertical-integration & displacement check
Zhiyuan/AgiBot is a robot OEM, not a motor/actuator manufacturer — no disclosed in-house hollow-cup-motor or joint-module capability found; buying merchant components is consistent with typical humanoid-OEM practice. Displacement risk: unassessed this fire (no evidence of AgiBot dual/multi-sourcing beyond Leili) — flag for next causal-rigor pass.

Base business (appliances/auto/industrial) is the real driver of the stock today; robotics optionality may take years to become financially visible, if ever.
