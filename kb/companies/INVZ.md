Flip to BUY: (a) a binding production contract or disclosed volume/dollar terms for the Daimler/Torc program, (b) confirmation of sole/preferred sourcing on that program, (c) Nasdaq cure resolved without a reverse split. Flip to KILL: going-concern language confirmed in the 20-F, or failure to cure by Sept 21 2026 triggering delisting.
