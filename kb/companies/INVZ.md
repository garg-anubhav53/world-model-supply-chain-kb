# Innoviz Technologies — INVZ (Nasdaq)
Updated 2026-07-07 (Fire 12) | Verdict: **KILL (downgrade from WATCH)** | Track: **AV**

**Fire 12 — downgraded to KILL.** The specific catalyst this WATCH was tracking (Daimler Truck/Torc Robotics binding conversion) has now gone 7 months (Dec 2025 → Jul 2026) with zero progress, while fundamentals deteriorated sharply and the delisting overhang remains unresolved. Full reasoning below.

- **Daimler Truck/Torc — still non-binding, confirmed no update.** Q1 2026 earnings call (May 14 2026) and the accompanying 6-K contain only development-stage language — management said they are "delivering units to support milestones through SOP," no contract terms, no volume commitments, no exclusivity. No 8-K/6-K/20-F amendment between April-July 2026 upgrades this relationship. Innoviz describes it as one of four L3/L4 programs (alongside BMW, VW/CARIAD, Mobileye) slated for 2026-2027 series production — and notably, it is the LEAST mature of the four, not the most. BMW (InnovizOne/Magna, already in series production for BMW's L3 program) and VW/CARIAD (InnovizTwo ramping for the ID. Buzz AD shuttle, large-volume program, 2026 fleet deployments) are Innoviz's real production anchors — but these are EXISTING, already-disclosed legacy programs already embedded in current (declining) guidance, not a fresh forced-multiplication catalyst. Innoviz's own 20-F risk factors describe the business as "substantially dependent" on the CARIAD design win — i.e., this is business-as-usual revenue, not upside optionality.
- **Financials deteriorated sharply.** Q1 2026 revenue $7.1M, DOWN from $17.4M YoY (~-59%); net loss $26.2M; missed both revenue and EPS estimates. FY2026 guidance reiterated at $67-73M (implies a big H2 ramp still unproven). Liquidity $60.1M, positive equity $59.7M — not distressed, but burning against a shrinking base.
- **Price (2-source, Jul 6 2026):** $0.6777 (stockanalysis.com) / $0.676-0.6926 (macroaxis/aggregation). 52wk range $0.537-$2.54 — down ~73% from high, near all-time lows. This is a DE-rating, not a re-rating — the stock has not "run," it has collapsed, consistent with deteriorating fundamentals rather than an unpriced asymmetry.
- **Delisting clock — still unresolved.** Second Nasdaq minimum-bid-price non-compliance notice (dated ~Mar 25-27 2026, sources vary by a day), 180-day cure to **Sept 21 2026**. Stock currently ~32% below the $1.00 cure threshold with ~11 weeks left; no reverse split or other cure mechanism has been announced. A reverse split (if used) would itself be a further dilution/distress-adjacent signal.
- **Verdict rationale (KILL):** The one gate this WATCH was explicitly conditioned on — a binding Daimler/Torc conversion — has not moved in 7 months and shows no sign of moving before Q3/Q4 2026 at the earliest. Meanwhile revenue nearly halved YoY, and an active, unresolved delisting clock adds real structural overhang. Compounding: even Innoviz's OWN more-mature programs (BMW, VW/CARIAD) are already fully reflected in the declining current-year guidance — there is no fresh forced-spend catalyst left to underwrite a WATCH, just an ongoing, shrinking base with one speculative long-dated (2028+) optionality thread.
- **What would flip this back to WATCH:** A disclosed Daimler/Torc production contract with actual $/volume terms or confirmed sole/preferred sourcing; OR a stabilization/reacceleration of core (BMW/VW) revenue with the delisting risk resolved without a dilutive reverse split. Absent either, do not re-diligence without a fresh, dated catalyst.
