- **What:** China e-bike/e-scooter maker, HK-listed. On 2025-11-18 signed a strategic cooperation agreement with 有鹿机器人/Youlu ("Deer") Robotics to become a supplier of "high-precision planetary reducer joint modules," first-batch order target >100,000 units for delivery in 2026.
- **Gate 1 (NOT re-rated): size gate FAILS, decisive on its own.** Market cap confirmed **~HK$4.6-6.2B (~$600-800M USD)** across 3+ independent sources (Investing.com 2026-07-08: HK$5.25B/$670M; Simply Wall St: ~HK$6.23B/$795M; Google Finance/360MiQ/Lixinger range HK$4.65-5.2B). Decisively above the ~$300M widened-bar WATCH cap and at/near the $800M hard auto-kill line, **regardless of buyer quality** — this alone is sufficient to kill.
- **Gate 2 (causal link / buyer legitimacy): Youlu is real but "humanoid" framing is stretched.** Youlu, founded 2023 by Dr. Chen Junbo (陈俊波, former head of Alibaba DAMO Academy's robotics lab), is a genuinely funded company (>RMB100M round, May 2024, Yuanjing Capital/Sinovation Ventures/Baidu Ventures) with real disclosed revenue (cumulative orders ~RMB600M/~$83M by March 2025 for its "Master 2000" embodied-AI brain product across cleaning/new-energy/logistics/construction-machinery use cases). BUT its actual shipping products are wheeled/mobile-form retrofits (cleaning vehicles, forklifts), not bipedal humanoids — its own roadmap lists "轮式机器人、复合式机器人、人形机器人" (wheeled → compound → humanoid) as sequential future stages, humanoid still forward-looking. The Luyuan deal itself is framed around "具身机器人" (embodied robots, the broader category); some press loosely labeled it "humanoid."
- **Verdict: KILL, size gate.** Moot regardless of the buyer-legitimacy question — Luyuan's market cap alone fails the gate. Youlu itself is legitimate and revenue-generating but not primarily a humanoid-robotics forced buyer as of today.
