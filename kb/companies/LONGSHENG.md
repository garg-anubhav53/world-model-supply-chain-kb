# Wuxi Longsheng Technology (无锡隆盛科技) — SZSE: 300680.SZ

**Verdict: PARK** (found-and-gate-checked Fire 22, 2026-07-07). Real Nidec contract-manufacturing relationship exists but is small/revocable; the load-bearing "Tesla Optimus" framing is unconfirmed rumor-tier. Not a supply-chain multiplier as currently evidenced — a China A-share "robotics-concept" pattern until a primary disclosure says otherwise.

## What they make
Legacy Tier-2/3 auto-parts supplier: EGR (exhaust gas recirculation) systems, NEV motor iron cores. Acquired Weihan (subsidiary, Mar 2025) to diversify into humanoid-robot harmonic/cycloidal reducers and joint modules. Also holds a minority stake in Shenzhen Diedong Tech (visual-tactile e-skin for dexterous hands) — not diligenced this fire.

## The claimed thesis (buyer chain: Tesla → Nidec → Longsheng)
- Oct 31 + Dec 12 2025 IR disclosures (互动易): "cooperation upgrade" with **Nidec** on harmonic-reducer R&D, capacity expansion, processing — retail/自媒体 press describes Longsheng as "the only listed China company Nidec directly partners with," implying exclusive contract-manufacturing status.
- Claimed component passed PPAP audit, supply slated H2 2025; capacity ramp ~70k sets by Q1 2026 → ~200k sets targeted H2 2026.
- Also claims engagement with XPeng, Seres/AITO, Agibot (智元), Luxshare — diversified buyer set.

## Why it's PARKED, not WATCH (Opus adversarial gate-check, Fire 22)
1. **Gate 1 (not re-rated): PASS.** ~¥24.9 (Jul 2026), down ~7%/12mo, fully round-tripped a mid-2025 hype spike (52-wk high ¥52.8-68.7 pre a Apr 2026 capital-reserve share conversion that mechanically halved the nominal price — verified this isn't a fresh crash).
2. **Gate 2 (causal link airtight): FAIL — binding gate.** The Longsheng↔Nidec relationship is real and dated. But **no Nidec or Tesla primary source confirms Nidec as a designated Optimus rotary-actuator/reducer Tier-1** — that link exists only in retail-forum chatter. Mainstream Optimus harmonic-reducer supplier maps assign that slot to Green Harmonic (绿的谐波), the only domestic maker to pass Tesla's automotive-grade cert. Longsheng's own Q3 filing doesn't name Tesla or Nidec, saying only "connected with a core Tier-1 of a well-known overseas customer." **Nidec also makes its own harmonic reducer in-house** (FLEXWAVE line, own Pinghu plant) — Longsheng is revocable overflow/contract-machining capacity, not a locked sole-source. High vertical-integration/insourcing risk.
3. **Gate 3 (revenue multiplies): PARTIAL.** Company's own language: robotics/reducer segment is "a small share of total revenue" vs. ~¥2.65B legacy base (FY2025 +10% YoY, but Q1 2026 -4% YoY, mature/slowing). The 200k-set ramp is an unfunded capacity plan, no confirmed offtake volume or ASP.
4. **Gate 4 (financial health): PARTIAL.** Healthy on paper (9M2025 revenue ¥1.81B +10%, net profit ¥210M +37%, ~11-12% net margin) but amber flags: acquisition-funded expansion (Weihan + a 微研/aerospace unit) via share issuance (dilution), goodwill build on a >¥4.2B asset base, and a mid-2026 capital-reserve conversion — a tool often used to sustain retail interest in concept stocks.

**Capture-rate:** weak/illusory for the Tesla thesis (~0% confirmed); possibly sole-source for Nidec's small China outsourced-processing volume, but easily insourced given Nidec's own reducer line next door.

**Bear case:** Textbook A-share robotics-concept construct — a mature, slowing auto-parts business bolted a robotics narrative onto itself via a bought-in subsidiary, fed vague IR-platform "cooperation upgrade" language that retail forums amplified into a two-hop Tesla story that no primary source confirms.

## Re-open trigger
A PRIMARY disclosure (深交所 filing or Nidec's own IR) confirming BOTH (a) contracted volume/revenue with Longsheng, AND (b) Nidec's actual Optimus/humanoid-actuator designation from Tesla or Nidec itself (not media/retail inference).
