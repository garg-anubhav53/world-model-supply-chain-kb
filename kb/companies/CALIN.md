# Calin Technology — TWSE: 4976 (佳凌科技)
Updated 2026-07-08 (Fire 30) | Verdict: **WATCH-weak / PENDING (leaning KILL — Waymo tie unconfirmed, revenue not multiplying)** | Track: **AV**

**Found via:** Waymo 6th-gen sensor-pod teardown (new D5 method). DigiTimes (2024-06-26, reinforced 2025-08-26/2025-09-22) reports Calin as "reportedly the sole supplier of Waymo's cameras."

- **What:** Taiwan automotive camera-lens maker (~$32M FY2025 revenue). Automotive/ADAS/LiDAR-lens segment = 30-40% of revenue, +22% YoY H1 2025, management guiding toward 50%.
- **Gate 2 — Causal link: WEAK, single-sourced, possibly stale.** The "sole supplier" claim lives ONLY in a DigiTimes headline with the hedge "reportedly" — the article body contains no attribution or supporting detail. Calin's own earnings-call materials (Fugle, 2025-09-18) never mention Waymo by name and disclose no customer concentration; when Chinese-language analyst coverage cites an AV demand driver for Calin, it names **Tesla Robotaxi**, not Waymo. No second outlet, no Calin IR confirmation, no teardown report corroborates the claim anywhere. **Staleness/spec-mismatch risk:** the 2024 report predates Waymo's 6th-gen Driver (Feb 2026 blog post), which moved to a 17MP custom imager on the Zeekr platform — Calin's mainstream lenses are 4-8MP (12-16MP still in development), so even if real, the relationship may not extend to the current platform. **Must be treated as an unresolved, material caveat, not established fact.**
- **Gate 1 — NOT re-rated: borderline pass.** Price ~NT$37.05 (2026-07-07), mkt cap ~NT$5.17B. Point-to-point 12m move ~-5%, but this masks an active rally: 52wk range NT$22.80-59.20, was NT$27.10 on 2026-04-03 → ~NT$37 now (+37% in ~3 months), with documented +33%-in-a-week surges on the AV-optics narrative. Not an undiscovered name — a recurring, volatile Taiwan theme stock.
- **Gate 3 — Revenue multiplication: WEAK, not evident in actuals.** Automotive segment growing, but company-level revenue is flat-to-down: FY2025 total NT$1.02B; 2026 5-month cumulative NT$433M, -0.18% YoY; May 2026 -4.53% YoY. Automotive gains are being offset by declining consumer/video/security segments — no top-line multiplication happening today.
- **Gate 4 — Financial health: income statement fails, balance sheet solvent.** FY2025 EPS -1.88; Q1 2026 EPS -0.49 (-206% YoY); gross margin collapsed to ~2%; FX loss NT$42.97M H1 2025. But balance sheet is healthy — liability ratio only ~16%, multi-year runway against ~NT$250M/yr burn. This is a profitability problem, not distress. The "2026 payoff" framing is analyst/scout inference, NOT management guidance — the Sept 2025 call gave no forward timeline, and 2026 actuals so far (flat revenue, continued Q1 loss) don't confirm a turnaround.
- **Adversarial bear case:** Waymo tie may be unconfirmed/stale/non-exclusive; ~2% gross margin despite automotive growth points to structural pricing/competitive pressure (Sunny Optical, Sekonix), not a temporary R&D phase; already a known speculative theme stock with repeated momentum spikes, not undiscovered.
- **Verdict: WATCH-weak/PENDING**, honestly leaning KILL on the thesis as stated. Keep only as optionality (clean balance sheet, multi-year runway, real if unconfirmed AV-optics exposure). **Upgrade triggers:** (1) a second independent confirmation of the Waymo relationship — ideally confirming 6th-gen-platform design-in; (2) gross margin recovery to double digits; (3) company-level revenue growth turning positive YoY.
