# Xinjian Transmission (新剑传动 / Hangzhou Xinjian Mechatronic Transmission) — pre-IPO, ChiNext

**Fire 43 update — STALENESS GUARD fired (held on the shortlist 6 fires, Fire 37-42, without a genuine from-scratch re-derivation — every check since promotion was a light "still accepted, no progress" status check). The re-derivation found TWO load-bearing claims from the original Fire 37 promotion were OVERSTATED:**

1. **"Named buyer is Tesla" — corrected to market inference, not a filed disclosure.** The actual ChiNext prospectus language (10jqka/Sina/OFweek, all citing the same filing) uses an **anonymized "Customer A" (A公司)** — "报告期内，公司与人形机器人行业标杆客户A公司建立合作关系" — plus unnamed 客户B-F. Tesla is **market inference** ("外界普遍认为是特斯拉"/"widely believed to be Tesla"), reinforced by press describing 客户A as "某整车企业" (a certain automaker) — narrows toward Tesla/Xpeng but is interpretation, not disclosure. The KB's "17.46%/50.72%" concentration figures trace to the **2022 NEEQ transfer document (871312)**, not the current ChiNext prospectus, and could not be re-confirmed as Tesla-attributed.
2. **"H2 2025 mass-production order, 1,000+ units" — corrected to unverified market rumor, likely overstated by ~9x.** The KB's own cited primary source (OFweek deep-dive) actually discloses **150 units/month capacity and 109 arm assemblies shipped** — not 1,000+. The 1,000-unit figure traces only to "多个独立信源/市场传闻" (market rumor), and is **partially contradicted**: Tier-2 sub-supplier Demax (德迈仕) publicly stated (ifeng, Jan 2026) it has "暂未与新剑合作该项目" (not yet cooperated with Xinjian on this project) when asked about the rumored large Tesla order. Audited reality: **¥31.11M total robotics revenue / ~109 assemblies in 2025**, not a confirmed 1,000+-unit PO.

**Capture rate — CONFIRMED 1-of-2 (dual-source), never sole-source.** Per eastmoney (2026-06-16)/21jingji: "新剑传动是特斯拉人形机器人行星滚柱丝杠的两个供应商之一" (one of two planetary-roller-screw suppliers). Corrects the prior framing of Wuzhou Xinchun (五洲新春/603667.SH, already-killed elsewhere in this KB) as a head-to-head rival — it is actually a **Tier-2 sub-supplier TO Xinjian** (semi-finished screw components, strategic agreement Mar 2025), though it separately builds its own humanoid-screw capacity and could compete later. Net: dual-source at minimum — blocks any BUY case, still permitted for WATCH.

**Multiplication math (proactively sized) — real only in the bull tail, negligible near-term.** Base: total revenue ¥383M (2025), robotics 8.19%/¥31.11M. At ~¥5-10k content/dexterous-hand assembly × 2 hands/robot × 50% capture: Tesla's own 2026 target (50-100k Optimus units, aspirational, repeatedly slipped per Electrek) implies ~¥0.5-1B (~1.3-2.6x current TOTAL revenue); the 2027 target (500k-1M units) implies ~¥2.5-5B (~6-13x the robotics line). The multiplier is real IF Tesla's ramp materializes AND Xinjian holds its 1-of-2 slot — but 2026 near-term reality (109 units) is a rounding error, and every leg of the bull case is a Tesla-execution bet, not a disclosed contracted mechanism.

**IPO timeline — NO progress since Fire 37's promotion.** Still "已受理"/accepted (2026-06-26 per SZSE listing.szse.cn), NOT yet in the 问询/inquiry stage as of this check (~13 days post-acceptance, so absence of an inquiry letter is expected, not itself a red flag — but zero procedural advancement in 6 fires). Realistic listing timeline, per ChiNext acceptance-to-committee averages (~191 days) plus registration/issuance, is **Q2-Q4 2027** — still technically inside the <18-month bar (before Jan 2028), but barely, and unconfirmed.

**Financial health — operationally solid, but poor investable target.** Revenue ¥263M→326M→383M (2023-25, ~+17%/yr), net profit ¥44.1M→57.5M→62M (profitable, ~16% net margin), 100+ patents, first domestic mass-producer of planetary roller screws. Red flags: **gross margin declining 34.04%→30.46%→29.70%**; core business remains mature auto-parts (precision metal 46.16%, auto-seat HDM drivers 27.95%) growing only mid-teens; a prior **NEEQ listing (2017) was voluntarily delisted Aug 2023** at only ¥184M revenue; and a severe **capex/raise-vs-scale mismatch** — ¥2.822B raise + ¥2.6B plant targeting 1M-unit capacity against a ¥383M revenue / ¥62M-profit base, entirely contingent on a Tesla ramp with no firm PO.

**Adversarial bear case (why this is now a DOWNGRADE, not a hold):** the name has no realistic path to BUY, only two exit states, and both fail. (a) If the IPO stalls (plausible — it's a NEEQ-delisting repeat-filer with an oversized raise and a rumor-driven story), it stays permanently un-investable, dead weight on the shortlist. (b) If it lists, it debuts on peak Tesla-Optimus/丝杠 hype — rumored valuation ¥20B, analyst "reasonable" ¥5-10B ($0.7-1.4B) — **2-9x over this KB's size cap and already fully priced**, the identical pattern to Unitree and other Tesla-adjacent China IPOs that price the entire story at debut (i.e., the "not-yet-priced" discount that justifies WATCH is likely ~zero by the time it's actually investable). The whole thesis rests on an anonymized "Customer A," a rumored-and-partially-denied 1,000-unit order, a dual-source slot, and aspirational Tesla volumes — remove any one leg and the multiplier collapses. The upcoming 问询 (inquiry) round is a live refutation risk: if the exchange forces disclosure and Customer A isn't Tesla, or the order is confirmed at ~109 units, the story breaks publicly.

**Four-gate score: 4/8** (genuinely re-derived down from an implicitly stronger score at promotion): Gate 1 (not-yet-re-rated) 2/2 — trivial pass, pre-IPO, no price exists (also the un-investability trap). Gate 2 (named forced buyer) 1/2 — real anonymized benchmark customer + genuine small robotics revenue, but Tesla is inference not disclosure, order magnitude is unverified/partly-denied rumor. Gate 3 (multiplication) 1/2 — real tail-multiplier, but 1-of-2 capture + Tesla-execution-gated + near-term negligible. Gate 4 (financial health/investability) 0/2 — profitable operating company but not investable (pre-IPO, no firm date), declining margins, oversized capex/raise mismatch, NEEQ-reject history, near-certain size-cap breach on listing.

**Verdict: DOWNGRADE.** Demoted from top-5 conviction to lowest-priority "track-only" forward-listing WATCH. **Pre-committed TERMINAL KILL trigger** (apply automatically at the next relevant event, no fresh re-derivation needed): KILL on IPO pricing if implied market cap exceeds the size cap (near-certain given ¥5-20B chatter vs. the $300-800M cap), OR if the 问询 round refutes the Tesla/Customer-A identity or confirms the order at ~100 units, OR if the filing stalls/withdraws. **Standing lesson reinforced:** the staleness-guard rule caught a case where the ORIGINAL promotion itself, not just a subsequent "no change" period, rested on treating a strong market inference (Tesla) and an unsourced rumor (1,000+ units) as confirmed named-buyer disclosures.
