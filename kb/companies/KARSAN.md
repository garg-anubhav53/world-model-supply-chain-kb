# Karsan Otomotiv — BIST: KARSN (OTC: KRSOF)
Updated 2026-07-08 (Fire 30) | Verdict: **KILL (bump not multiply, multi-vendor, loss-making + Turkish macro risk)** | Track: **AV (autonomous shuttle/transit)**

**NEW GEOGRAPHY: Turkey/BIST — first-ever touch in this KB.** Found via ImportYeti customs bill-of-lading (Beep Inc importing "Karsan Jest Class Autonomous Minibuses," dated 2026-05-10), independently corroborated (Karsan IR, Sustainable Bus, PRNewswire, Beep's own product page).

- **What:** Established 60-year Turkish commercial-vehicle OEM (minibuses/buses, contract mfg for Peugeot/Citroën/Renault Trucks/Hyundai). 2025 revenue ~€330M (+11% YoY), 67% electric. NOT primarily an autonomous company — autonomy is a rounding error on the P&L today.
- **Gate 1 — NOT re-rated: technical pass, real-terms fail.** Price ~12.89 TL, 12m move +27-28.7% nominal (2-source). But Turkish CPI ran ~31-35% in 2025/~32% in 2026 — in real/hard-currency terms this is roughly a 5% LOSS, a quiet de-rate, not hidden upside. Value-trap signature.
- **Gate 2 — Causal link: confirmed but weak.** Real Beep relationship (Adastec L4 stack, Damera NA distributor), corroborated beyond customs. NOT exclusive — Beep is an explicit multi-vendor operator (Navya, Holon, ZF, Benteler, Oxa-powered platforms too). Commitment = 10 units (pilot scale), H2 2026 delivery timed to FIFA 2026, no dollar figure disclosed. At ~€400-500k/unit, ~€4-5M ≈ ~1% of revenue.
- **Gate 3 — Revenue multiplication: FAILS decisively.** Autonomous fleet today = 10 US units + ~16 European pilot deployments (Rotterdam, Stavanger, Gothenburg) — operationally impressive, commercially trivial. Karsan itself makes no claim autonomy becomes material; 2026 guidance (80% EV mix, ~700 EV units, >2,800 fleet) is not autonomy-driven. Full driverless type-approval not sought until 2027. No disclosed order pipeline beyond the initial 10.
- **Gate 4 — Financial health: weak.** Net loss 676M TL for 9M-2025 despite gross margin improving to 22.9%; rising net debt, liquidity flagged as a risk. Turkish macro overlay (~32% inflation, vulnerable lira) is a risk factor this KB's other names don't carry.
- **Adversarial bear case:** autonomous shuttles have been "5 years away" for a decade (Navya, Beep's former supplier, went into distress; EasyMile struggled); no lock-in (multi-vendor); core business commoditized; 10-unit pilot may never scale beyond a FIFA-timed showcase; currency/leverage can erase hard-currency returns even if the Turkish business grows nominally.
- **What IS genuinely interesting (not investable via KARSN):** Karsan+Adastec is arguably the global leader in full-size Level-4 autonomous buses actually in revenue passenger service (first RDW-approved autonomous airport bus at Rotterdam, first driverless tunnel transit in Europe at Stavanger). **Adastec (private) is the more thesis-relevant actor to trace if it ever lists** — it owns the autonomy stack, not Karsan.
- **Verdict: KILL** as a revenue-multiplication equity thesis. Score 3/10. Log Turkey/BIST as a newly-touched geography (see coverage-ledger D2); watch for Adastec going public as the sharper follow-up.
