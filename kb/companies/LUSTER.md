# Lingyunguang / LUSTER LightTech (凌云光, STAR Market: 688400.SH)

**VERDICT: KILL (found-and-gate-checked Fire 24, same-fire)**
**Node:** data/software (motion-capture/machine-vision) · **Track:** R (robotics, adjacent) · **Geo:** China A/STAR

## What they sell
Machine-vision inspection systems (core business, consumer-electronics QC) + FZMotion, an optical motion-capture system built by wholly-owned subsidiary 元客视界 (Yuanke Vision), co-marketed with Unitree as an "embodied-intelligence robot research & training solution."

## The claim
Media/company statements (cls.cn, Sohu, 163, Feb 2025 - ongoing) describe FZMotion as used in Unitree's R&D/training pipeline; surfaced via a fresh media wave around Unitree's STAR Market IPO going effective Jul 6 2026.

## Why KILLED — fails on 3 independent gates (any one alone would be binding)
1. **GATE 1 (price) — FAIL, binding.** ~66.22 CNY (Sina/Futu, May 24 2026), +84.56%/12m (fupanwang) — up from ~22-23 CNY (Nov 2024) — roughly 3x in ~18 months. Est. P/E ~180x. Already a fully-priced "宇树概念股" (Unitree-concept stock) since Feb 2025.
2. **GATE 2 (causal link) — FAIL.** FZMotion is an R&D/training-data-collection TOOL, not a shipped per-unit component — same product is pitched to Siasun, drones, unmanned vehicles generically (Futu). **Unitree's own STAR IPO prospectus does NOT name Lingyunguang/FZMotion** — Unitree is highly vertically integrated (in-house motors/reducers/encoders/dexterous hands/lidar; external sourcing only ~14-18% of cost, mostly optional lidar/hands). Not in the per-unit BOM.
3. **GATE 3 (multiplication) — FAIL.** FY2025 group revenue 2,911.7M CNY (+30.35%), dominated by consumer-electronics machine vision + the JAI camera acquisition. FZMotion sits inside 元客视界, a subsidiary that grew only 28% H1 — FZMotion is roughly ~1% of group revenue. One-time capital-equipment sale to an R&D lab, not a per-unit-shipped part — does not scale with Unitree unit volume.

**GATE 4 (financial health) — PASS (weak):** profitable (net profit 161.4M CNY, +50.7%) but thin ~5.5% net margin, rising receivables, JAI-acquisition goodwill-impairment risk flagged by analysts.

**Capture-rate: LOW** — FZMotion competes directly with Xsens, OptiTrack, Vicon, domestic mocap vendors; no sole/preferred lock, a terminable co-marketing arrangement not a supply contract.
**Displacement risk: HIGH** — training-data mocap is being displaced by teleoperation/markerless capture/synthetic data; Unitree's vertical integration means trivial in-house substitution.

Don't re-diligence without: (a) a direct disclosure in Unitree's OWN filings naming Lingyunguang as a supplier (not just co-marketing), AND (b) evidence FZMotion converts to a per-unit-shipped product rather than a one-time R&D tool sale, AND (c) a price retrace back under the +60%/12m line.
