# Genesis / CIGIT / 创世纪 — 300083.SZ (ChiNext, Guangdong Genesis Intelligent Equipment)
Written 2026-07-08 (Fire 33) | Verdict: **KILL — first-ever full gate-check.**

**What:** CNC machine-tool maker (3C drilling-milling centers are its core product); mid-cap industrial capital-equipment company. Previously logged PENDING as a real, company-confirmed Unitree machine-tool supplier with stale (~Feb 2025) disclosure never independently gate-checked.

**Market cap (2-source):** 2026-07-08 ¥12.92, total cap **¥21.5B (~$2.97B)** (fupanwang, dated 2026-07-08); baseline ¥9.32, cap **¥15.36B (~$2.1B)** (earlier check) — price up ~39% since January 2026.

**Primary-source Unitree evidence (real, but explicitly a fungible capex sale, not a robot component):** 互动易/IR record (投资者关系活动记录表 2025-001, filed 2025-03-03): supplying Unitree since 2022; Unitree is *"人形机器人领域重要的长期合作客户之一"* (an important long-term customer in the humanoid field) — **but the same record explicitly states "合作限于数控机床领域，公司未直接供应人形机器人零部件"** ("cooperation is limited to the CNC machine-tool field; the company does NOT directly supply humanoid-robot components"). This is a company selling Unitree a general-purpose CNC machine tool ("selling the pickaxe"), not a bespoke, physically-necessary humanoid component.

## Gate scores (0-2 each)
1. **NOT re-rated — 0/2. KILL.** Market cap ~$3B — roughly 10x this KB's $300M widened-bar WATCH cap — and already up ~39% in 2026 on the humanoid concept.
2. **Causal link — 1/2.** Confirmed, dated relationship since 2022, but the company itself frames it as a commodity capital-equipment sale, fungible against DMG Mori, Haas, and domestic CNC peers — no physically-necessary or exclusive link to humanoid production specifically.
3. **Revenue MULTIPLIES / materiality — 0/2. KILL.** FY2025 revenue **¥5.32B (~$734M)**; Unitree/humanoid-specific share is undisclosed but is necessarily a rounding error within a large general-purpose machine-tool business. No plausible path for humanoid-forced spend to multiply a ¥5.3B base.
4. **Financial health — 0/2. KILL.** Rising leverage (asset-liability ratio 41.9%→51.8%→53.79% over 2023-25); FY2025 net profit **-39.6%** YoY; a **December 2025 court judgment holding Shenzhen CIGIT jointly liable for ¥382M**; serial private placements ("capital thirst"); 8 regulatory penalties in the past 5 years.

**Total: ~1/8.**

**Capture-rate:** Weak/non-exclusive — machine tools are commodity capital equipment; Unitree is one of many buyers and Genesis is one of many vendors, zero lock-in.

**Adversarial bear case:** This is a ~$3B mid-cap selling generic CNC machines, one of which Unitree happens to buy — the company's own disclosure walls this off from any bespoke humanoid-component relationship. Meanwhile the balance sheet is actively deteriorating: a ¥382M litigation judgment, rising leverage, repeated capital raises, and a multi-year pattern of regulatory penalties. It fails simultaneously on size, materiality, and financial health.

**Verdict: KILL.** Fails size, materiality, and financial-health gates; the Unitree link is real but fungible and immaterial.
