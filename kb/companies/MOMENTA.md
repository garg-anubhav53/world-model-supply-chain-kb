# Momenta Global — HKEX: 6880
Updated 2026-07-07 (Fire 21, found-and-diligenced same-fire) | Verdict: **KILL / PARK (WATCH-weak leaning KILL)** | Track: **AV**

**Fire 21 — found via idle-dimension trigger scan (marketplace/data business model), full Opus gate-check same-fire:** Chinese autonomous-driving/ADAS software-and-hardware stack provider. HKEX IPO priced late Jun/early Jul 2026, ~$751M raised at ~$9B valuation, listing effective **Jul 8, 2026** (ticker 6880) — not yet trading as of this diligence pass (Jul 7). 14 cornerstone investors incl. Mercedes-Benz, BlackRock funds, GIC, Fidelity — retail tranche oversubscribed **414x**. 2025 revenue ~RMB2.4bn (~$337M), +82% YoY.

- **Gate 1 — NOT re-rated: FAILS.** Private-market valuation already jumped $6B (Sept 2025) → $9B (IPO, Jul 2026) in ~9 months — a 50% markup pre-IPO. At $9B/~$337M revenue = **~27x trailing sales** at the offer price, before any first-day pop (near-certain given 414x oversubscription + 14 locked-up cornerstones holding ~50% of the deal). This is priced for perfection, not undiscovered.
- **Gate 2 — Causal link: PASSES, verified.** Real, concrete production programs (not vague partnership language), cross-checked via TechNode/Automotive News/Gasgoo/Reuters: Mercedes-Benz (CLA launched fall 2025, next-gen S-Class production 2027, up to 40 models), BMW (Neue Klasse/iX3 LWB 2026), Audi (E5 Sportback 2025), BYD (DIPAI JV, shareholder since 2017), SAIC (Mpilot on IM-brand). 130+ models signed, 900,000+ vehicles deployed. No per-contract dollar/volume figures disclosed.
- **Gate 3 — Revenue multiplies: PASSES on mechanism.** Per-vehicle software-licensing model: licensing revenue up 42x in 2 years (RMB23M→RMB968M), gross margin 17.5%→71.6%. Near-zero marginal cost per incremental vehicle designed into high-volume Western platforms = real operating leverage. No disclosed fixed royalty-per-unit rate.
- **Gate 4 — Financially healthy: PASSES clearly.** ~RMB10bn (~$1.4B) cash at YE2025, ~$2B+ pro forma post-raise. Headline RMB3.46bn 2025 net loss is ~RMB2.8bn non-cash (preferred-share fair-value marks converting at IPO) — adjusted net loss only ~RMB303M, narrowing.
- **Capture-rate / vertical-integration — the thesis-fit problem:** Momenta is a **multi-sourced, software-only Tier-1** — BMW simultaneously partners with Huawei; OEMs run parallel vendors + in-house stacks. Customer concentration is FALLING BY DESIGN (top-5 = 62.6% of 2025 revenue, down from 86.7% in 2023) — the opposite of the "one concentrated forced buyer" this thesis targets. **Structural headwind:** industry data shows software-only-vendor new contracts fell 37% YoY (2024) while full-stack players (Huawei ADS, Horizon, Mobileye, Bosch) gain share. BYD and GM (both named customers) are also building in-house stacks that could displace Momenta.
- **Comp check:** Momenta ~27x trailing sales vs. Mobileye (MBLY) ~3.5x sales ($6.7B mcap/$1.9B rev) — Mobileye itself down ~47%/52wk, a cautionary re-rating precedent for hyped ADAS names.
- **Adversarial bear case (survives):** the IPO valuation already bakes in the multiplication story (27x sales, 414x book, $6B→$9B in 9 months) — there is no unpriced edge. Diversified/falling customer concentration means no single forced buyer who can't walk away. Software-only positioning is losing share structurally. HKEX-listed China name carries geopolitical/access friction.
- **Verdict: WATCH-weak / PARK, not added to pipeline.** Clears the quality gates (2-4) cleanly but fails the two gates that matter most for this thesis: already re-rated (gate 1) and no concentrated forced buyer (capture-rate). **Binding gate to revisit: valuation.** Upgrade path = a post-listing de-rating (flat/down first week, or a subsequent pullback to a materially lower sales multiple) COMBINED with a disclosed program-level sole-source deal with per-unit royalty terms on a high-volume Western platform (e.g., locked S-Class/Neue Klasse economics). **Action: check the Jul 8 2026 open and first-week close before re-considering.**
