Serial issuance has clearly ACCELERATED versus the prior ~7x-in-5-months read, not stabilized.

- **Net read:** this is opportunistic, not distressed, dilution — raises are being done at rising prices ($13.50, above the reverse-split-era lows) into a now-large cash cushion, and solvency risk fell further even as mechanical dilution accelerated. Still, share-count growth this fast can cap upside even with a growing contract book. **Maintain WATCH; re-check share count and cash every fire; the DARPA/SpaceWERX figures no longer need re-verification (now primary-confirmed), but any future NASA JSC dollar claim should be treated as false unless a new primary source appears.**
