# Momentus Inc. — MNTS (Nasdaq)
Updated 2026-07-07 (Fire 17) | Verdict: **WATCH (NEW — first space-vertical name in the KB)** | Track: **space robotics/autonomy (adjacent vertical)**

- **What:** Space-robotics integration/flight-proof micro-cap (~$50-70M mkt cap). Provides in-space robotic-arm/on-orbit-assembly integration and flight demonstration services; the robotic-arm hardware itself is sourced from a private partner (Lodestar Space) — Momentus is the systems-integration + flight-proof layer, not the arm manufacturer.
- **Gate 1 — NOT re-rated:** PASSES. Price ~$6.36-6.58 (Yahoo Finance / market aggregator, 2-source, Jul 7 2026), down sharply from the 52wk high ($43.55, pre-reverse-split-adjusted figures need reconciling with a cited $113.39 post-split high — flag for next-fire cleanup). Down, not up — clears the not-yet-rerated bar easily.
- **Gate 2 — Causal link:** PARTIAL/CONFIRMED-MIXED. SpaceWERX/Space Force $1.86M Direct-to-Phase-II SBIR (Sustained Space Maneuver Challenge) CONFIRMED via primary company IR release. DARPA NOM4D Phase 3 relationship is real (initial award Apr 2024, $1.2M in milestones since Mar 2025, on-orbit demo NET Feb 2026 via SpaceX Transporter-16) but the specific "~$4.2M total" figure is an unconfirmed secondary-aggregator number, not found in a primary line-item. NASA JSC contract exists (Flight Opportunities/STMD study, Aug 2025 PR) but the "Fly Foundational Robots" naming and a larger follow-on award are unverified in primary sources — NASA's decision was pending "in September" per the original PR. **Action: re-confirm DARPA/NASA dollar figures directly next fire before treating them as hard facts.**
- **Gate 3 — Revenue multiplication:** PASSES on paper. $10.0M 2026 revenue guide vs. $1.1M 2025 (~9x), confirmed via company 8-K/shareholder letter (May 2026) — a genuinely tiny base with a steep ramp.
- **Gate 4 — Financial health / solvency (THE binding gate, now CLEARED):** Q1 2026 10-Q (filed ~May 2026) states that as a result of financings completed since 12/31/2025, "substantial doubt [about going concern] no longer exists." Cash rose to $23.5M at 3/31/2026 (from $12.8M at YE2025); Q1 operating burn was ~$5.8M (~$1.9M/month) — implying >12 months runway even before an additional $5.0M private placement + ~$10.5M ATM raise in May 2026 (pushing cash to an estimated $30M+). **However this clearance was bought via aggressive, ongoing dilution**: a 1-for-17.85 reverse split (effective 12/17/2025, ~25M→~1.4M shares) was followed by share count re-inflating **~7x in five months** (1.4M→5.84M→9.99M, Dec 2025→Mar 2026→May 2026) via ATM sales, warrant inducements, and private placements. Shareholders REJECTED a further authorized-share increase at a Feb 2026 special meeting while approving warrant-related dilutive proposals — press described this as "dilution fatigue." Convertible/junior secured notes also outstanding (small, ~$1.6M principal).
- **Verdict: WATCH (new add).** Solvency gate formally clears per the most recent 10-Q, and the causal story (real government contracts, tiny re-rated-down base, steep revenue ramp) is genuine. **Binding risk to monitor: dilution, not distress** — this is a serial ATM/warrant issuer, and further share-count inflation could cap upside even if the underlying contract book grows. Re-check cash/share-count every fire; downgrade if a new reverse split or going-concern language reappears. Upgrade path: a confirmed, larger, multi-year NASA/Space Force award with a primary-source dollar figure, or evidence Lodestar Space's arm hardware is itself scaling toward a public listing (would open a 2nd space-vertical name).
