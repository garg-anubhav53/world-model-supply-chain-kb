# Arbe Robotics — ARBE (Nasdaq)
Updated 2026-07-07 (Fire 13) | Verdict: **WATCH (early-stage, binding gate showing time-decay — watch closely)** | Track: **AV**

**Fire 13 update:** Price $0.7864 (Jul 6, stockanalysis.com) / $0.7837 (Jul 4, Yahoo Finance) — still >20% below the Nasdaq $1 compliance threshold with the Oct 12 2026 cure deadline now 3 months out and no organic path visible (a reverse split looks increasingly likely). **No new signed backlog figure disclosed since the ~$1M reported Mar 31 2026** — the binding capture-rate/backlog gate has now shown zero quantified movement across 2 consecutive fires (Fire 12, Fire 13), echoing the Innoviz "time-alone" failure pattern (ROUTINE lesson: track how many fires a gate stays open, not just its current state). One incremental positive: a NEW, separate Hirain design win — a China state-owned automaker selected Hirain's radar (built on Arbe's 48x48 chipset) for an L4 program, SOP Dec 2026, volume production 2027; Hirain is also co-developing a 2nd (24x12-channel) platform with Arbe for China. Real, but pushes recognition further into 2027, doing nothing to resolve the near-term backlog gate or the Nasdaq clock. **Not a KILL yet** (genuine incremental design-win activity, not silence) but flagging explicitly: if Oct 12 2026 passes without compliance AND the next fire still shows no backlog quantification, downgrade toward KILL should be actively considered rather than extended again.

- **What:** Israeli 4D imaging radar chipset maker. Genuinely tiny base: FY2025 revenue $1.03M, FY26 guide $4-6M. Price $0.786-0.82 (2-source: stockanalysis.com + Robinhood/search, Jul 6 2026), mkt cap ~$96.5-101M, 52wk range $0.552-$2.88.
- **Gate 1 — NOT re-rated:** PASS. -39.3%/12m (stockanalysis.com) — this is a DE-rating, not a re-rating; the +114% move often cited is only off the Mar 2026 all-time low ($0.55), not a 12-month comparison. Still ~19x trailing P/S ($96M/$1.03M) — expensive on fundamentals despite the de-rate.
- **Gate 2 — Causal link:** PARTIAL. Real named design wins: (a) Hirain (China state-owned-automaker Tier-1 integrator) selected Arbe's LRR610 chipset for an L4 robotaxi program, SOP Dec 2026, chipset shipments already commenced (PRNewswire, Dec 29 2025) — but no dollar terms disclosed; (b) "follow-on orders" from unnamed global robotaxi players for the Phoenix 360° sensing product (Q1 2026 earnings call) — customer still unnamed; (c) US Army autonomous-logistics, "hundreds of units," small. Vertical-integration/displacement risk LOW — 4D imaging radar is a merchant-supplied category (Bosch/ZF/Aptiv/Continental/Uhnder/Zendar/Arbe all compete for it); L4 robotaxi fleets are radar-heavy (Waymo ~6 radars/vehicle, Cruise ~21) and Tesla's vision-only stance was never a radar-buyer scenario, so the category itself is not being displaced.
- **Gate 3 — Revenue multiplication:** WEAK SUPPORT. Backlog at Mar 31 2026 was only **$1M** vs the $4-6M FY26 guide — guidance is largely design-win pipeline, not signed backlog. Content economics: chipset ~$50-150/radar × several radars/robotaxi ≈ $300-600/vehicle; Hirain's "thousands" of L4 units in 2027 is only a low-single-digit-$M event — meaningful vs the tiny base but not transformational on its own. Real upside requires the mass-market 24×12 platform hitting China L2+/L3 volume (millions of units) — unsigned, years out.
- **Gate 4 — Financial health:** CAUTION, not distress. Cash $53.6M (Mar 31 2026, incl. an $18.5M Q1 registered-direct raise); FY26 adj-EBITDA loss guide $(28)-$(31)M; runway ~1.5-1.8yrs but **funding model is serial dilution** (two raises within a year). **Nasdaq minimum-bid deficiency notice (Apr 13 2026) — must regain $1 by Oct 12 2026** or face a reverse split/delisting risk. No going-concern language found.
- **Capture-rate — the binding gate:** UNFAVORABLE. No evidence of sole-sourcing anywhere in the disclosed relationships. Hirain is a Tier-1 integrator that can and likely does qualify multiple chipset vendors; Uhnder is a direct chipset rival, and Bosch/ZF/Continental are entrenched incumbents with far larger balance sheets. Arbe is one-of-several on every named program.
- **Adversarial bear case:** decade-long pre-revenue promotional story with repeated dilution; sub-$1 delisting clock; $1M backlog cannot underwrite a $4-6M guide; every named end-customer relationship is either unnamed (robotaxi Phoenix orders) or a Tier-1 integrator that isn't the end-buyer (Hirain); no proof of sole-source in a market of much larger, better-capitalized competitors.
- **Verdict: WATCH (early-stage), not yet a BUY.** Not a KILL because the category is safe from displacement, the base is genuinely tiny, the stock has de-rated not re-rated, and multiple real (if small/unnamed) design wins exist. **Binding gate: backlog/PO conversion.** Flip to BUY: (a) signed POs lifting backlog materially toward the $4-6M guide, (b) a named end-customer with disclosed volume (not just a Tier-1 integrator relationship), (c) Nasdaq compliance resolved without value-destructive dilution.
