# Baida Precision / 百达精工 — SSE: 603331.SH
Created 2026-07-08 (Fire 32) | Verdict: **KILL** (found-and-killed same fire) | Track: **R**

**Found via:** EDGAR+互动易 China A-share sourcing sweep. Taizhou-based traditional compressor & auto-parts maker (est. 1995) that formed a robotics JV in late 2025.

- **Gate 1 (NOT re-rated): AMBIGUOUS/UNVERIFIED for the full 12mo window.** The stock ran from ~¥10 to a limit-up close of ¥19.80 on 2026-02-13 (~+98% over ~3-4 weeks) on the robotics-JV news, then pulled back after the company's own 2026-02-24/25 clarification announcement downplayed the robotics revenue. Full 12-month (Jul 2025→Jul 2026) % move could not be pinned down from 2 independent sources this fire — flag as UNKNOWN, don't treat as cleanly passing.
- **Gate 2 (causal link): PASSES the disclosure-quality bar, but is thin.** Named buyer: **Galbot (银河通用/Beijing Galaxy General Robot Co.)**, an embodied-intelligence/general-purpose robot maker. Source is the COMPANY ITSELF (互动易 post + cninfo announcement dated 2025-11-26 announcing a JV — 台州百银通用机器人有限公司, Baida holding 40%, RMB6M registered capital — to develop core components for embodied-intelligence robots), plus a 2025-12-22/23 announcement of a 1,000-unit robot deployment. This clears the "company said it, not an investor" bar from the Laimu lesson. But the deployment is described as **Galbot robots being deployed INTO Baida's own precision-manufacturing lines** (compressor/auto-parts production) — i.e., Baida is a robotics CUSTOMER/pilot-site in that specific announcement, with the JV's actual component-supply business still nascent.
- **Gate 3 (revenue multiplies): FAILS — decisive gate.** Company's own clarification announcement (~2026-02-24/25) states robotics-linked business revenue is currently **"极低" (extremely low)** and "尚未形成规模化销售" (has not yet achieved scale sales). Same filing discloses FY2025 net profit swinging to a loss of **~-RMB193.33 million**. There is no disclosed content-$/unit or committed order volume for the JV's actual component business — the 1,000-unit figure is robots Galbot is deploying at Baida, not units of Baida-made components sold to Galbot.
- **Gate 4 (financially healthy): FAILS.** FY2025 net profit swung to a loss of ~-RMB193.33M per the company's own disclosure — a legacy compressor/auto-parts business under real stress, not a fortress balance sheet funding an early-stage robotics optionality bet.
- **Adversarial bear case:** This is a legacy industrial-parts maker whose stock popped ~2x on a vaguely-scoped robotics JV announcement, which the company itself then walked back within weeks ("revenue extremely low, no scale sales yet") while simultaneously disclosing a large net-loss swing. The clearest read of the primary sources is that Baida is currently a Galbot robot CUSTOMER (buying/piloting robots for its own factory automation) with an early-stage, unpriced JV bet on someday supplying components back — not yet a forced/named component supplier capturing multiplying humanoid-buildout revenue. Real disclosure, real named counterparty, but fails materiality (Gate 3) and financial-health (Gate 4) decisively as of today.
