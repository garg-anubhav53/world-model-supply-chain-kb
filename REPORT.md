## Standing lesson this fire
**Two new standing lessons.** (1) A real, shipping product with a real customer order that the company is contractually barred from naming is a distinct dead-end shape from "sample-stage, no orders yet" — call it "NDA-gated real disclosure" — and it deserves a standing recheck flag (an instant trigger if the name ever leaks) rather than being treated as a closed kill the same way an unproven or denied relationship would be (Dongfang Electric Heating, Zhongshi Technology).
